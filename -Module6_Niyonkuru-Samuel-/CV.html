ABOUT US

WELCOME TO SAM KITCHENWARE LTD

SAM KITCHENWARE LTD is owned and managed by Mr. NIYONKURU Samuel. It is oriented towards kitchen tools manufacturing sector specialises in the design, supply, instillation, support and lifecycle maintenance of commercial Kitchen and catering equipment. We have provided commercial kitchen design solutions and commercial kitchen installation for a number of commercial establishments such as hotels, restaurants, schools and bars and remote camps.The company is located at Cyimana in Gasabo District, Kigali City and it works in Damange House on road KG 588.
